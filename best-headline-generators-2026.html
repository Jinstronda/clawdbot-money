‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free headline generator?+
HookSmith is the best free headline generator in 2026. It generates 10 headlines per click using 50+ proven formulas, supports multiple content types and tones, and requires no signup or API key. It's completely free with no usage limits.
Do headline generators actually work?+
Yes, headline generators work as brainstorming tools and starting points. They use proven copywriting formulas (curiosity gaps, how-to structures, power words) that have been tested across millions of headlines. The best approach is to generate multiple options and then customize your favorite for your specific audience.
What makes a viral headline?+
Viral headlines typically include: a curiosity gap (readers want to know more), emotional triggers (surprise, awe, humor), specific numbers (7 tips, 23% increase), power words (ultimate, proven, secret), and relevance to a trending topic. The best headline generators incorporate these elements into their formulas.
Should I use a headline generator or write my own?+
Use both. Generate 10-20 headline options with a tool like HookSmith, then pick the best 2-3 and customize them. This gives you the creative spark of proven formulas combined with your unique voice and audience knowledge. Professional copywriters often brainstorm 25+ headlines before choosing one.
